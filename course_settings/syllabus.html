Unit 3 Web Development

Unit Description:

In this unit, we will build on skills learned in previous units by developing secure and well-constructed CRUD applications. We will look into the role of business logic design in developing web applications and explore three different ways of implementing those designs within a react application. We will focus on thought processes and methodologies to build web applications within the workplace throughout the unit.
