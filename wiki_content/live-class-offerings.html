Sprint Reviews 1-2 are 30-minute live experiences taught by instructors that are targeted to help you grasp major concepts found in the sprint challenges.

Ask an Expert

Ask an Expert sessions are 30-minute live experiences staffed by an instructor. Are you stuck on a module and just can't seem to connect the dots? Have a question, and the online searches are getting you nowhere? These are purposefully designed to get you unblocked and unstuck in the BloomTech curriculum.
