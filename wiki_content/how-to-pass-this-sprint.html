How to Pass This Sprint

Technical Sprint Requirements Required

  • Sprint Challenge (100% Required)

    A time-bound project that you must complete using the skills you've learned in each Sprint. After your first attempt, you will have unlimited attempts to achieve a passing grade of 100%.

  • Sprint Assessment (80% Required)

    A multiple-choice test that checks your understanding of the Sprint's content. After your first attempt, you will have unlimited attempts to achieve a passing grade of 80%.

ACS Sprint Requirements Required

  • General Code Assessment (GCA)

    Attempt the official GCA test

  • Career Readiness

    Complete the corresponding career readiness assignment

Instructions are provided in the respective sprints on what you must submit for each sprint challenge.
